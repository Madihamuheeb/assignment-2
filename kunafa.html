kunafa


ingredient list

  • 6 pieces L’usine Sliced Bread
  • 4 Kiri Square Portion
  • 100 grams (1 cup) lotus cream
  • 100 grams (1 cup) fresh raspberries

cooking steps

  • Preheat the oven on 220°C
  • Add melted butter and mix well
  • n a baking dish, use half of the dough in the bottom and flatten it with your hands
  • Put on top the second half of the dough and flatten it over the cheese
  • Pour the warm sugar syrup on top
